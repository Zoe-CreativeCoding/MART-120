For this unit, we returned to Code.org. But instead of making characters dance or drawing shapes, we were instead creating rudimentary games.

We used some of the same ideas that we had in the Scratch project in order to make our games. We took the characters, dialouge, and background to make a world, then added interactivity to make a game. The player is able to move at least one character and can gain or lose points based on different factors.
